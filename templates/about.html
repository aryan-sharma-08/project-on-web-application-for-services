Our Story

R.K. Studio was founded with a simple vision — to capture life's most precious moments with creativity, passion and professionalism. Over the past 10+ years, we have grown from a small photography studio to a full-service media and printing company serving clients across Jaipur, Dausa and all of Rajasthan.

We believe every event tells a unique story. Whether it's a grand wedding, a corporate event or a simple family gathering — we bring the same dedication and attention to detail to every project we undertake.
